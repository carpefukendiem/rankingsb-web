### The Dual Market of the Santa Ynez Valley

Every Santa Ynez Valley business serves two distinct customer types:

**Tourist/visitor market:** Weekend travelers from LA, San Diego, and the Bay Area. These customers discover businesses primarily through Google searches — "best restaurant Buellton," "wine tasting Los Olivos," "things to do Solvang." They're often high-spend, spontaneous, and driven almost entirely by Google search and reviews.

**Local resident market:** Year-round residents of the Valley who need all the same services as any community — medical, dental, legal, home services, auto repair, specialty retail. These customers also use Google, but value familiarity and trust over novelty.

An effective SEO strategy addresses both simultaneously.
