### Why Pet Services SEO Has Unique Characteristics

Pet owners have specific trust requirements that shape how they search and decide:

- They read reviews obsessively — a negative review about a dog being injured at a boarding facility is catastrophic for a business
- They look for photos of the facility, the staff with animals, and the actual environment
- They often search for specialty services ("fear-free vet Santa Barbara," "small breed groomer Ventura")
- Emergency searches ("emergency vet open now near me") require immediate visibility and fast-loading mobile pages

An effective SEO strategy for pet service businesses needs to address all of these factors — not just rankings, but the full profile that converts an anxious pet owner into a booked appointment.
