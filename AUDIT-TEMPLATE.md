## 📝 HOW TO FILL OUT (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Company Info (1 min)
- Fill in header section
- Google the company
- Note their website

### Step 2: Rankings (2 min)
- Search "[service] Santa Barbara"
- Find their position (or note if not ranking)
- Check Google Maps

### Step 3: Competitors (2 min)
- Who ranks #1, #2, #3?
- Note their review counts
- Note their photo counts

### Step 4: Find 3 Issues (3 min)

**Common Issue #1:** Not ranking in top 10
- Impact: "75% of clicks go to top 3 results"

**Common Issue #2:** Incomplete Google Business Profile
- Impact: "Incomplete profiles rank 30% lower"

**Common Issue #3:** No recent reviews
- Impact: "Google prioritizes businesses with fresh reviews"

**Common Issue #4:** Website not mobile-friendly
- Impact: "Google penalizes non-mobile sites"

**Common Issue #5:** No location pages
- Impact: "Can't rank for '[service] [city]' searches"

### Step 5: Quick Wins (1 min)

**Common Quick Win #1:** Add 5 photos to GBP
- Time: 5 minutes

**Common Quick Win #2:** Update business description
- Time: 10 minutes

**Common Quick Win #3:** Ask 3 customers for reviews
- Time: 15 minutes

### Step 6: Calculate Impact (1 min)

**Formula:**
- Industry average: [service] calls worth $XXX each
- If they get 5 more calls/month = $X,XXX
- 10 more calls/month = $XX,XXX

**Be conservative** - don't overpromise

## 💡 PRO TIPS

### 1. Lead with Competitor Comparison
- "Here's who ranks #1... and why they shouldn't"
- Triggers competitive instinct

### 2. Be Specific
- "Position #8" not "you don't rank well"
- "3 reviews in 6 months" not "not enough reviews"

### 3. Show Easy Wins
- "This takes 10 minutes" = low barrier
- "You can do this today" = immediate action

### 4. Calculate ROI
- Always show potential revenue
- "At $500 per call..."
- Make it tangible

### 5. Keep it Visual
- Boxes, lines, checkmarks
- Easy to scan
- Looks professional
