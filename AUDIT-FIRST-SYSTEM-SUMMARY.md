## 🔄 THE AUDIT-FIRST WORKFLOW

### STEP 1: COLD CONTACT (Offer Audit)
**Time:** 2 minutes
**Goal:** Get permission to run audit

**Script:**
```
"I'm offering free SEO audits for [industry] companies in Santa Barbara. 
I review where you rank, check your website, and compare you to 
competitors. Takes 10 minutes. Worth it to see what we find?"
```

**Result:** YES → Schedule review call (or email audit)

---

### STEP 2: RUN AUDIT (10 Minutes)
**Time:** 10 minutes
**Tools:** 10-MINUTE-AUDIT-CHECKLIST.md

**Research:**
1. Google rankings (2 min)
2. Website check (2 min)
3. GBP review (3 min)
4. Fill template (3 min)

**Output:** Completed 1-page audit report

---

### STEP 3: DELIVER AUDIT (15-20 Minutes)
**Time:** 15-20 minutes
**Tools:** AUDIT-TEMPLATE.md (printed)

**The Call:**
1. **Rapport** (2 min): "How's business?"
2. **Present findings** (5 min): Walk through audit page by page
3. **Reveal 3 issues** (5 min): Specific problems with impact
4. **Show competitor gap** (2 min): "They're getting your calls"
5. **Offer solution** (3 min): "We fix all 3 for $1,995"
6. **Close** (3 min): "Ready to move forward?"

**Result:** Close deal OR schedule follow-up

---

### STEP 4: CLOSE OR NURTURE
**If YES:**
- Get payment info
- Schedule kickoff
- Commission: $997

**If MAYBE:**
- Address concerns
- Send proposal
- Follow up in 2 days

**If NO:**
- Stay in touch monthly
- Add to nurture list
