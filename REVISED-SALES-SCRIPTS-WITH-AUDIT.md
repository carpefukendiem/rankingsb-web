## 🔍 CONDUCTING THE AUDIT (10 Minutes)

### Step 1: Google the Company (2 min)

**Search queries:**
- "[Company Name] Santa Barbara"
- "[service] Santa Barbara"
- "[service] near me" (while near their location)

**Document:**
- Where do they rank? (Page 1, 2, 3, not at all?)
- Do they show up in Google Maps?
- What does their GBP look like?
- Who ranks above them? (Competitors)

### Step 2: Check Their Website (3 min)

**Quick review:**
- Mobile-friendly? (Google's #1 ranking factor)
- Page speed? (slow = bad rankings)
- Title tags optimized? (should include location + service)
- Contact info easy to find?
- Do they have location pages?

### Step 3: Review GBP (3 min)

**Check:**
- Complete profile? (description, hours, services)
- Categories selected?
- Photos recent? (last 30 days?)
- Posts? (weekly updates?)
- Reviews? (quantity, rating, recency)
- Website link working?

### Step 4: Identify Quick Wins (2 min)

**Common findings:**
- ❌ Not ranking for main keywords
- ❌ GBP incomplete (missing description, categories)
- ❌ No photos uploaded
- ❌ Not posting updates
- ❌ Website slow on mobile
- ❌ Missing location pages
- ❌ No recent reviews
- ❌ No FAQ section
