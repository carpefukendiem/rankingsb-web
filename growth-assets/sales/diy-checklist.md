# DIY Local Lead Checklist (Printable)

## If you do nothing else this week, do these 10 things

### Google Business Profile (GBP)
1) Confirm primary category is correct + add 5–10 relevant secondary categories.
2) Add services with short, customer-friendly descriptions.
3) Add 15–30 high-quality photos (team, trucks, before/after, shop).
4) Post 2 updates this month (offer + proof).
5) Ask for 5 new reviews (and reply to every review).

### Website
6) Put your phone number + primary CTA above the fold on mobile.
7) Create/upgrade 1 “money service page” with: offer, proof, FAQs, process, and a strong CTA.
8) Add a simple trust section: licenses/insurance, guarantees, years in business, reviews.

### Follow-up
9) If you miss a call, text back within 60 seconds (automation is best).
10) Follow up 5 times across 10–12 days for every lead that doesn’t book.
