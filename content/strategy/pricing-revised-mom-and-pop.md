### Objection Handling

**"That's a lot of money"**

```
"I get it. Let's look at what you're spending now.

If you're doing any Google Ads, you're probably paying $10-20 per click, right? And maybe 1 in 10 clicks calls you?

So you're paying $100-200 per phone call.

With our system, you're paying $297/month for unlimited calls. No per-click charges. And unlike ads, when you stop paying Google, you disappear. With this, you own the rankings.

Plus, we break it into two parts — the $1,995 gets everything built. The $297 is just maintenance after that.

How many extra customers per month would you need to make this worth it?"
```

**"What if it doesn't work?"**

```
"Fair question. Here's our guarantee: If after 90 days of being fully optimized, you haven't improved your Google rankings at all, we'll keep working for free until you do.

But here's the thing — I've never had to do that because this works. We just need you to commit to the process.

The reason we charge upfront is so we can do the deep work needed to actually get results. If we did this dirt cheap, we'd have to cut corners and you wouldn't see results.

Make sense?"
```

**"Can we just do the monthly part?"**

```
"I wish we could, but here's why we don't: The setup work is where 80% of the results come from. That's building your foundation, optimizing everything, creating your presence.

The monthly is just keeping it running — like maintaining a car. But you can't maintain something that wasn't built in the first place.

We've tried lower upfront costs, and honestly, clients who don't invest upfront don't stick around long enough to see results. They cancel after 2 months.

The clients who invest upfront? They see the value, they stick around for years, and they refer their friends. That's the model that works.

So yes, it's $1,995 to get started, then $297/month. Does that work for you?"
```
