**What could go wrong?**

### Risk 1: Articles Don't Rank
**Mitigation:**
- We target low-competition long-tail keywords
- Local specificity = less competition
- Build backlinks (I'll guide Sal on this)
- Adjust: Focus on paid traffic if organic is slow

### Risk 2: Sales Rep Doesn't Perform
**Mitigation:**
- 90-day trial period
- Weekly check-ins (catch problems early)
- Clear metrics (data doesn't lie)
- Adjust: Reassign leads, adjust scripts, or part ways

### Risk 3: You Can't Keep Up
**Mitigation:**
- Content is written (you just publish)
- Sales calls can be 2-3 per day, not 10
- Can defer to Sal after Month 2 (he handles more)

### Risk 4: Clients Don't Pay/Stay
**Mitigation:**
- 3-month minimum contracts
- Month-to-month after (ongoing pressure to perform)
- Focus on industries with recurring needs
- Results-based pricing (tie to their revenue)
