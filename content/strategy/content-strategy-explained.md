## Content Types (Your Arsenal)

### 1. Industry + Location Articles (Primary)
**Examples:**
- "HVAC SEO in Santa Barbara"
- "Plumbing SEO in Ventura County"
- "Dental Marketing for Santa Barbara Practices"

**Goal:** Rank when "[industry] marketing [location]" is searched

### 2. Service Area / City Pages
**Examples:**
- "Digital Marketing in Goleta"
- "SEO Services in Montecito"
- "Marketing Agency Serving Carpinteria"

**Goal:** Rank for "marketing agency [city]"

### 3. Problem/Solution Content
**Examples:**
- "Why Your Plumbing Website Isn't Getting Calls"
- "How HVAC Companies Can Survive Slow Seasons"
- "The Real Cost of Poor SEO for Dentists"

**Goal:** Rank for pain-point searches

### 4. Comparison/Guide Content
**Examples:**
- "Best Marketing Agencies for Home Services in Santa Barbara"
- "SEO vs. Google Ads for Plumbing Companies"
- "How to Choose a Marketing Agency for Your HVAC Business"

**Goal:** Rank for "best" and "how to choose" searches (high intent)
