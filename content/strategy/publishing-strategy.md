## Step-by-Step Publishing Instructions

### Step 1: Create Funnel/Website (One Time)

**In GoHighLevel:**
1. Dashboard → Sites → Funnels (or Websites)
2. Create New Funnel
3. Name: "Industry Landing Pages"
4. Type: Blank or "Service" template
5. Domain: Connect your custom domain

### Step 2: Create Industry "Thank You" Page Template

**Each industry page needs:**
- Industry-specific headline
- Article content (below)
- Trust elements (logos, testimonials)
- Lead capture form (sidebar + bottom)
- Clear CTA

**Page Template Structure:**
```
[Header with navigation]
↓
[Hero: Industry + Location headline]
   "Plumbing SEO in Santa Barbara: Get More Emergency Calls"
↓
[Subheadline]
   "How Santa Barbara plumbing companies can rank higher in Google and capture high-value emergency service calls"
↓
[Trust bar: "We've helped 50+ plumbing companies..."]
↓
[Article content] ← YOUR CONTENT GOES HERE
↓
[Inline CTA box: "Free Plumbing SEO Audit"]
↓
[More article content]
↓
[Large bottom CTA]
   Headline: "Ready to Get More Plumbing Calls?"
   Sub: "Get a free SEO audit and find out why you're not ranking"
   Button: "Get My Free Audit" → Links to form
↓
[Footer]
```

### Step 3: Create Lead Capture Form

**In GHL:**
1. Dashboard → Forms → New Form
2. Name: "Industry SEO Audit Request"
3. Fields:
   - Name (required)
   - Email (required)
   - Phone (required)
   - Company (required)
   - Website (optional)
   - Industry (dropdown: HVAC, Plumbing, Roofing, Solar, Appliance, Other)
   - Business Type (Commercial/Residential)
4. Submit Action:
   - Send to: Ruben + Sal
   - Add to pipeline: "SEO Audit Requests"
   - Trigger email sequence: "Audit Request Received"
   - Redirect: Thank you page

### Step 4: Publish Each Article

**For each article (using Plumbing as example):**

1. **Create new page:**
   - Funnel → Add Step
   - Name: "plumbing-marketing-santa-barbara"
   - Path: /industries/plumbing-marketing-santa-barbara

2. **Configure SEO:**
   - Page Title: "Plumbing SEO in Santa Barbara | Get More Emergency Calls | Rankingsb"
   - Meta Description: "Discover proven SEO strategies for Santa Barbara plumbers to rank higher in Google and capture more emergency service calls from local homeowners."
   - OG Image: [Create in Canva]

3. **Add content sections:**
   - Copy article content from my file
   - Break into sections with H2 headers
   - Add inline images (stock or screenshots)

4. **Insert lead capture:**
   - Add form in sidebar (desktop)
   - Add inline CTA after intro paragraph
   - Add large CTA section at bottom

5. **Set up automation:**
   - Email immediately: "Thanks! We'll audit your site within 24 hours"
   - Task for Ruben: "New plumbing lead - call within 2 hours"
   - Tag: "Plumbing Industry Lead"

6. **Test everything:**
   - Submit test form
   - Check email notifications
   - Verify pipeline entry
   - Test mobile view

7. **Publish:**
   - Set status to Published
   - Add to navigation menu
   - Submit to Google Search Console (for indexing)
