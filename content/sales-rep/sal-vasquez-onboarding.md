### Step 3: Pipeline Configuration

**Create pipeline: "Sales Funnel"**

**Stages:**
1. **Lead** (new inquiry)
   - Entry: Form submission, referral
   - Exit: Contact attempted

2. **Contacted** (called, no answer)
   - Action: Log call, schedule follow-up
   - Exit: Spoke with prospect

3. **Connected** (had conversation)
   - Action: Qualify using BANT
   - Exit: Qualified or disqualified

4. **Qualified** (meets criteria)
   - Criteria: Has budget/authority/need/timeline
   - Action: Schedule appointment
   - Exit: Appointment booked

5. **Appointment Scheduled** (meeting set)
   - Action: Confirm 24hr before
   - Exit: Showed or no-show

6. **Appointment Complete** (held meeting)
   - Action: Send proposal
   - Exit: Proposal sent

7. **Proposal Sent** (awaiting decision)
   - Action: Follow up 2-3x
   - Exit: Won or Lost

8. **Closed Won** 🎉
   - Commission trigger
   - Onboarding handoff

9. **Closed Lost** ❌
   - Log reason
   - Long-term nurture

## Sales Scripts for Key Scenarios

### Script 1: Cold Call Opening

**Goal:** Get to qualification/appointment in under 5 minutes

```
"Hi [Name], this is Sal Vasquez with Rankingsb. 

[Pause - wait for acknowledgement]

I'm the local rep here in Santa Barbara, and I was looking at [Company]'s website. I noticed you're not ranking on page 1 for [keyword]—is getting more [industry] leads from Google something you're focused on this year?

[If yes] Great. We've helped [industry] businesses like [Example] get 30-50% more calls within 90 days. I'd love to show you what we found in a quick audit. Do you have 20 minutes this week for a brief call?

[If no] Totally understand—most [industry] owners I talk to are slammed. Quick question: Where are most of your customers coming from right now?

[Conversation continues...]

Look, I don't want to take up more of your time right now. How about we schedule a brief 15-minute call where I can show you what your competitors are doing and whether there's an opportunity? Would Tuesday or Thursday work better?"
```

### Script 2: Objection Handling

**"I already have someone handling my marketing"**
```
"Great—sounds like you're already investing in growth. Most of the [industry] owners I talk to have someone. Here's my question: Are you happy with the results? Are you getting enough calls to keep your [team/schedule] full?

[Pause]

If there's even a chance we could get you 20-30% more leads, would it be worth a 15-minute conversation to explore? No pressure—just information."
```

**"I need to think about it"**
```
"Of course—it's a decision that affects your business. What specifically are you weighing? Is it the investment, the timing, or something else?

[Address specific concern]

How about this: Let me send you a brief proposal by [day], and we can hop on another quick call [day+3] to answer any questions. Would that work?"
```

**"Send me some information"**
```
"Happy to. Just so I send you the right info—are you primarily looking for [service 1] or [service 2]? And roughly how many [customers/jobs] are you hoping to add per month?

[Take notes]

Perfect. I'll send this over by end of day. Can we schedule a quick follow-up for [day] to make sure I answered your questions?"
```
