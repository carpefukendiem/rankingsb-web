## QUALIFICATION QUESTIONS (BANT)

Ask these before pitching:

1. **Budget:** "Are you currently spending money on marketing? How much?"
   - Green flag: Spending $500+/month on ads
   - Red flag: "I don't believe in marketing"

2. **Authority:** "Who makes the marketing decisions for [Company]?"
   - Need to speak with decision maker

3. **Need:** "What's your biggest challenge getting customers right now?"
   - Green flag: "Not enough calls", "Relying on referrals"
   - Red flag: "We're too busy already"

4. **Timeline:** "When would you want to see results?"
   - Green flag: "ASAP", "This quarter"
   - Yellow flag: "Maybe next year"

**If 3+ green flags → Pitch Growth tier aggressively**
