## Lead Sources (Priority Order)

1. **Website Inquiries** (hottest) — Same-day response
2. **Content Lead Magnets** — I'll generate SEO traffic → forms
3. **LinkedIn Prospecting** — Search: "[Industry] Santa Barbara"
4. **Referrals** — Ask every existing client for 2 introductions
5. **Cold Email** — Targeted lists I build (industry + city + revenue signals)

---

## Qualification Questions (BANT+)

1. **Budget:** "Are you currently spending on marketing? How much?"
2. **Authority:** "Who makes the marketing decisions?"
3. **Need:** "What's your biggest challenge getting customers right now?"
4. **Timeline:** "When would you want to see results?"
5. **Local:** "How important is showing up in Google Maps for [city]?"

**Green flags:** Currently spending on ads, frustrated with current agency, just opened/rebranded
