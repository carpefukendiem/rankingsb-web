# GoHighLevel Publishing Guide — Rankingsb Content Strategy

## Current Setup
- Platform: GoHighLevel (GHL)
- Website: www.rankingsb.com
- Goal: SEO blog + location pages for lead generation

---

## Publishing Options

### Option 1: GHL Blog (Recommended)
**Pros:** Built-in SEO, fast, integrates with forms/CTAs
**Cons:** Less design flexibility

**How to publish:**
1. GHL Dashboard → Sites → Blog
2. Add New Post
3. Copy/paste article content
4. Add featured image (I can generate or suggest)
5. Set category: "Local SEO" or "[Industry]"
6. Add CTA at end: "Get a free SEO audit →"
7. Publish

### Option 2: Funnel/Website Pages
**Best for:** Location pages, industry-specific landing pages

**How to publish:**
1. GHL Dashboard → Sites → Funnels or Websites
2. Add Page
3. Use "Blog Post" or "Article" template
4. Copy content, customize
5. Add form for lead capture
6. Set SEO metadata (title, description)

---

## Content Types to Create

### 1. Industry + City Articles (Primary)
Format: "[Service] SEO for [Industry] in [City]"
Example: "HVAC SEO for Santa Barbara Heating Companies"

**Structure:**
- Hook: Local problem
- Why SEO matters for this industry
- Local search stats
- 5 tips specific to [city]
- CTA: Free audit

### 2. City Landing Pages
Format: "Digital Marketing in [City]"
Example: "Digital Marketing in Montecito, CA"

**Purpose:** Rank for "marketing agency [city]"

### 3. Comparison/Guide Posts
Format: "Best [Service] in [City]" or "How to Choose..."

## SEO Optimization in GHL

### On-Page SEO Checklist
- [ ] Title tag: 50-60 chars, includes keyword
- [ ] Meta description: 150-160 chars, compelling
- [ ] H1: Main keyword
- [ ] H2s: Subtopics
- [ ] Internal links: 2-3 to other pages
- [ ] Image alt text: Descriptive, keyword if natural
- [ ] URL slug: Short, includes keyword

### Schema Markup (If available in GHL)
- LocalBusiness schema
- Service schema
- Article schema for blog posts
