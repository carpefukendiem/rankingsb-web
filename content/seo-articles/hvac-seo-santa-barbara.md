## The 5 Pillars of HVAC SEO in Santa Barbara

### 1. Google Business Profile Optimization (Your #1 Priority)

Your Google Business Profile is the single most important asset for local HVAC SEO. Here's how to optimize it:

**Complete every field:**
- Business description with keywords: "HVAC repair Santa Barbara," "heating and cooling Goleta," "AC service Montecito"
- All 10 service categories (Primary: HVAC contractor, then add Air conditioning repair service, Heating contractor, etc.)
- Service areas: List every city you cover (Santa Barbara, Goleta, Isla Vista, Montecito, Summerland, Carpinteria)
- Hours: Include emergency service availability
- Products/services: Detail every offering with descriptions

**Post weekly updates:**
- Seasonal maintenance reminders
- Special offers ("$50 off AC tune-up")
- New equipment spotlights
- Customer testimonials

**Photo strategy:**
- Upload 5+ photos monthly
- Show: team, trucks, completed installations, before/after work
- Geo-tag images with Santa Barbara locations

**Review generation:**
- Aim for 2-3 new reviews per week
- Respond to every review within 24 hours
- Use keywords naturally in responses ("Thanks for trusting us with your Santa Barbara home's AC repair")

### 2. Location-Specific Landing Pages

Don't just have one "Service Areas" page. Create dedicated pages for each city you serve:

- /hvac-repair-santa-barbara-ca
- /air-conditioning-service-goleta
- /heating-repair-montecito
- /hvac-contractor-carpinteria

Each page should include:
- Unique content (300+ words) about that specific area
- Local landmarks and references
- City-specific service offerings
- Embedded Google Map
- Local customer testimonials
- Emergency service availability for that area

**Example intro for Goleta page:**
"Goleta homeowners know the unique challenges of coastal California living — from salt air corrosion on outdoor units to the specific permitting requirements for HVAC installations in Santa Barbara County. Our Goleta HVAC technicians live and work in your community, providing fast response times to neighborhoods like Kellogg, Storke Ranch, and the UCSB area."

### 3. Technical SEO for HVAC Websites

**Site speed matters:**
- Compress images (HVAC units, team photos)
- Use fast hosting
- Target under 3-second load time

**Mobile optimization:**
- 70%+ of emergency searches happen on mobile
- Click-to-call buttons must be prominent
- Simple forms for after-hours requests

**Schema markup:**
- LocalBusiness schema with service area
- Service schema for each offering
- FAQ schema for common questions

**Key pages every HVAC site needs:**
- Emergency service page (optimize for "24 hour HVAC repair Santa Barbara")
- AC repair/services
- Heating repair/services
- Maintenance plans
- Duct cleaning
