## Technical SEO for Carpet Cleaning Sites

### Site Speed (Critical for Mobile)
Carpet cleaning customers are often searching on mobile (emergencies). Your site must load in under 3 seconds.

**Quick Wins:**
- Compress before/after photos
- Use WebP format
- Lazy load images
- Minimize plugins
- Use a CDN

### Mobile Optimization
- **Click-to-call buttons** prominently displayed
- **Simple booking forms** (name, phone, service needed)
- **Fast-loading images**
- **Easy navigation** (3 clicks max to book)

### Schema Markup
Add LocalBusiness schema to your homepage:
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "Your Carpet Cleaning Company",
  "image": "https://yoursite.com/logo.jpg",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "streetAddress": "123 Main St",
    "addressLocality": "Santa Barbara",
    "addressRegion": "CA",
    "postalCode": "93101"
  },
  "telephone": "+1-805-XXX-XXXX",
  "priceRange": "$$",
  "areaServed": "Santa Barbara, Montecito, Goleta, Carpinteria"
}
```
