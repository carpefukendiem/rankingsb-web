## Google Business Profile for Multi-Location

### Option 1: Single GBP (Service Area Business)
If you have one physical location but serve multiple cities:
- Set primary category: Carpet Cleaning Service
- Add service areas: All Ventura County cities you serve
- Post about different service areas regularly
- Get reviews mentioning specific cities

### Option 2: Multiple GBPs (Physical Locations)
If you have multiple offices:
- Create separate GBP for each location
- Use unique phone numbers
- Get reviews for each location
- Link to city-specific landing pages

### Posting Strategy for Multi-Location
Rotate your Google Posts to cover different cities:
- **Monday:** Ventura-focused post
- **Wednesday:** Oxnard/Camarillo post
- **Friday:** Thousand Oaks area post

### Review Generation by Location
Ask customers to mention their city in reviews:
- *"Great service in Camarillo!"*
- *"Best carpet cleaner in Thousand Oaks"*
- *"Saved our Ventura beach house carpets"*
