# Rankings SB — Client Support OS (v1)

Date: 2026-02-01
Owner: Ruben

## Goal
Fix the perception problem (slow replies / “nothing is happening”) by standardizing **fast, consistent communication**.

This OS is designed for a front‑loaded service (SEO work mostly upfront) + ongoing GoHighLevel platform value.

## What we implemented in GoHighLevel (Agency level)
### Conversation Snippets (Text)
Created under **Conversations → Snippets**:

1) **ACK – Got it (EOD/Next Biz AM) — Ruben**

> Got it — thanks for the message. I’m on it. I’ll update you by end of day (or next business morning). — Ruben

2) **ACK – Need 1 detail — Ruben**

> Got it. Quick question so I can solve this fast: ____ ? I’ll update you by end of day (or next business morning). — Ruben

3) **ACK – Received / Checking — Ruben**

> Got it — I’m going to check this now and circle back with next steps. — Ruben

## How to use (2‑tap rule)
Whenever a client pings you and you can’t answer immediately:
1) Use one of the **ACK** snippets within 10–60 seconds.
2) Schedule the real reply (same day or next business morning).

This prevents the “lag looks bad” problem without sending automated messages to clients.
