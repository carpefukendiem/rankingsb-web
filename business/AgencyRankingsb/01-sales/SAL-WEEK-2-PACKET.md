## 🗣️ HVAC-SPECIFIC TALKING POINTS

### Seasonal Angles

**Winter (Now - Feb/March):**
- "Heating season is your busy time - are you capturing all the emergency calls?"
- "When someone's heater breaks at 10 PM, they grab their phone and search. Are you there?"

**Spring (Coming up):**
- "AC season prep starts now. Smart companies book maintenance calls in spring."
- "When that first hot day hits, your phone should be ringing off the hook."

### Industry Pain Points

1. **Emergency vs Maintenance Calls**
   - "You're probably getting lots of emergency calls but missing the maintenance contracts. SEO brings both."

2. **Service Area Confusion**
   - "Are you showing up in Goleta searches? Carpinteria? Or just Santa Barbara? We can fix that."

3. **Competition from Big Chains**
   - "How do you compete with [big chain]? They have bigger ad budgets. But local SEO levels the playing field."

4. **Seasonal Slowdowns**
   - "What happens in slow seasons? With consistent SEO, you get steady calls year-round."

### Objection Handlers

**"I get all my business from referrals"**
> "Referrals are gold - keep those. But what happens when referrals slow down? Or when you want to expand? SEO is your backup plan, your growth plan, and your recession insurance."

**"I tried SEO before and it didn't work"**
> "That's frustrating. Can I ask who you used? [Listen] Yeah, a lot of agencies overpromise and underdeliver. We focus on local service businesses only, and we guarantee you'll rank on page 1 for your main keywords in 90 days, or we work free until you do."

**"I'm too busy for more jobs"**
> "That's a good problem! But busy seasons don't last forever. While you're busy, we build your online presence so when things slow down, you have a queue of calls waiting. It's like filling a reservoir while it's raining."
