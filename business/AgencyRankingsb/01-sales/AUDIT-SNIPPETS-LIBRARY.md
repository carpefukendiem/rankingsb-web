## How to Use These Snippets

1. **Choose the right angle** based on what you found in research
2. **Personalize the bracketed parts** with their specific info
3. **Practice once** before the call so it sounds natural
4. **Have it in front of you** during the call (print or on screen)
5. **Adapt as needed** - these are starting points, not scripts to read verbatim
