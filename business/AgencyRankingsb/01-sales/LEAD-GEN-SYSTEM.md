## 📞 OUTREACH SCRIPTS

### Script 1: Cold Call (Audit-First Approach)

**Goal:** Book 15-minute audit review

```
Hi [Name], this is [Sal] with Rankingsb. 

I'm calling because I was looking at [Company] online and noticed 
some opportunities to help you get more [service] calls from Google. 

We're a local SEO agency working exclusively with [industry] companies 
in [City], and I wondered if you'd be open to a quick conversation.

Specifically, I'd like to run a free 10-minute audit of your online 
presence and show you exactly where you're losing potential customers 
to your competitors. 

No pitch, just insights you can use either way. Do you have 15 minutes 
this week for me to share what I found?
```

**If YES:** Schedule audit review call
**If NO:** Send audit report via email anyway

## 🔄 OUTREACH SEQUENCE

### Day 1: Initial Contact
- Cold call → Voicemail (if no answer)
- Immediately send email with audit

### Day 3: Follow-Up
- Email: "Did you get my audit?"
- Include one additional tip/insight

### Day 7: Check-In
- Call again
- Text if no answer: "Quick question about [Company]"

### Day 14: Value Add
- Send case study from similar business
- Subject: "How [Similar Company] got 40% more calls"

### Day 30: Final Attempt
- Email: "Should I close your file?"
- Either get response or archive lead

### Ongoing: Re-engagement
- Add to monthly newsletter
- Re-contact in 90 days with new offer/case study

## 🎯 LEAD SCORING

### Hot Leads (Call Immediately)
- Mentioned wanting more leads/calls
- Asked about pricing
- Currently running ads (spending money)
- New business (0-2 years old)
- Recently moved/expanded

### Warm Leads (Follow Up Weekly)
- Took audit but didn't buy
- Said "call me next quarter"
- Asked for more info
- Referral from current client

### Cold Leads (Nurture Monthly)
- No response after 5 touches
- Said "not interested" but didn't hang up
- Out of budget right now
- Too small (1-person operation)

## 🚫 OBJECTION HANDLING

### "I don't have time"
> "Totally understand—you're busy running a business. That's exactly why our clients hire us. They don't have time to figure out Google either. This call is just 15 minutes to see if it makes sense. If not, no harm done."

### "I can't afford it"
> "Fair enough. Let me ask—how much is a new [service] job worth to you? [Let them answer] So if we got you just 2 extra jobs a month, you'd break even. Most clients see results in 60-90 days. Does that timeline work?"

### "I'm already doing SEO"
> "Great! That means you understand the value. Can I ask—are you happy with the results you're getting? [If yes] Good for you. [If no or hesitant] Would you be open to a second opinion? I'll show you what we're seeing, no obligation."

### "I get all my business from referrals"
> "Referrals are gold—keep doing what works. But what happens when referrals slow down? Or when you want to expand? Having a steady stream of Google leads gives you predictability. We're not replacing referrals, we're adding to them."

### "I need to think about it"
> "Absolutely, it's a decision. What specifically do you need to think through? [Let them answer] Got it. How about this—I send you the agreement now, you review it, and I'll call you [specific day] to answer any questions. Fair?"
