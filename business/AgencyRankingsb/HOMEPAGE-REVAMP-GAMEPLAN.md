**Purpose:** Stop the scroll, make the promise, get the click

**Current word count target:** 15-25 words total

**Template:**
```
[HEADLINE - 6-8 words]
Get More Customers From Google

[SUBHEAD - 10-15 words]  
SEO for local service businesses. Rank #1 for "[service] near me" searches.

[CTA BUTTON - 3-4 words]
```

**Why this works:**
- Immediately says WHO (local service businesses)

- One CTA = no decision paralysis

## 🎨 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### 1. WHITESPACE IS YOUR FRIEND
- 60% of screen should be empty space
- Text blocks: max 2-3 sentences
- Breathing room between sections

### 2. ONE CTA, EVERYWHERE
- Every section ends with "Get Free Audit"
- Same button style throughout
- Same color, same text

### 3. MOBILE-FIRST
- 70% of traffic is mobile
- Test on your phone first
- Thumb-friendly buttons
- Readable without zooming

### 4. SPEED MATTERS
- Page must load in <3 seconds
- Compress images
- Minimal scripts
- No auto-playing videos

### 5. SCROLL DEPTH = INTEREST
- Hero: 100% see it
- Problem: 80% see it
- Solution: 60% see it
- Proof: 50% see it
- CTA: 30% see it

Make each section work for the skimmers.

## 📝 COPYWRITING RULES

### DO:
- ✅ Lead with benefits, not features
- ✅ Use "you" and "your" (customer-focused)
- ✅ Specific numbers ("15-20 calls" not "more calls")
- ✅ Short sentences (10-15 words max)
- ✅ Active voice ("We get you to #1" not "#1 rankings are achieved")
- ✅ One idea per paragraph

### DON'T:
- ❌ Industry jargon ("backlinks," "schema markup")
- ❌ Multiple CTAs competing
- ❌ Long paragraphs (3+ sentences)
- ❌ Passive voice
- ❌ Feature dumps ("We do X, Y, Z, A, B, C...")
- ❌ Generic claims ("best SEO company")
