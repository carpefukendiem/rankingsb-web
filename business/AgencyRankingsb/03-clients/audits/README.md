## 📝 HOW TO USE THE AUDITS

### Step 1: Open the File
```
File naming: ##-company-name-audit.txt
Example: 01-positively-electrical-audit.txt
```

### Step 2: Research (10 minutes per audit)

**What to Google:**
1. `"electrician [city]"` — find their ranking
2. Search their company name — find their website
3. Check their Google Business Profile
4. Look at top 3 competitors

**What to fill in:**
- `[CONTACT NAME]` — who you spoke to
- Position # and page
- Competitor names, reviews, photos
- 3 positive findings
- Specific issues you found
- Commission tracking details

### Step 3: Customize

**Make it specific to them:**
- ❌ "You don't rank well" 
- ✅ "You're at position #12 for 'electrician Santa Barbara'"

- ❌ "Your website is slow"
- ✅ "Your site takes 6.2 seconds to load on mobile"

### Step 4: Print & Deliver

**Best paper:** White or cream cardstock (not regular printer paper)  
**Color:** Yes, print in color  
**Format:** One page only (front only)

4. **Calculate ROI** — always show potential revenue
5. **Keep it visual** — use the boxes and lines already in the template
