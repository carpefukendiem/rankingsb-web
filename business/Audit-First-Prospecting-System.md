# AUDIT-FIRST PROSPECTING SYSTEM
## Rankingsb Cold Outreach Strategy (New)

**Concept:** Lead with value. Send audit first, follow up with call.

---

## 🎯 THE PROCESS

### Step 1: Research Prospects (Spike/Johnny 5)
- Find businesses with clear SEO gaps
- Prioritize: No website, bad reviews, page 2+ rankings
- Generate 20 prospects/day

### Step 2: Run 10-Minute Audit (GHL)
- Use Rankingsb Audit Generator
- Key metrics: GBP completeness, review count, keyword rankings, website speed
- Create 1-page PDF report

### Step 3: Send Audit (Email/SMS)
- Subject: "Quick audit for [Business Name] - 3 issues found"
- Body: "Hi [Name], I ran a quick audit on [Business] and found 3 things costing you search visibility. Report attached. Happy to walk you through it—no obligation. -Ruben"
- Attach: 1-page audit PDF

### Step 4: Follow-Up Call (Same Day or Next Morning)
- "Hi [Name], this is Ruben from Rankingsb. I sent you an audit yesterday—did you get a chance to look at it?"
- "I'm local in Santa Barbara. Happy to stop by and review it in person if easier."
- Goal: 15-min conversation, pitch service

### Step 5: Close or Nurture
- Hot prospects: Schedule full audit review
- Warm prospects: Add to nurture sequence
- Cold prospects: Monthly check-in

## 🔍 PROSPECT RESEARCH CRITERIA

### Ideal Prospects (A-Tier)
- ✅ No website OR very old website
- ✅ Google Business Profile incomplete
- ✅ Fewer than 10 reviews OR bad rating (<4.0)
- ✅ Ranking page 2+ for main keywords
- ✅ Service area business (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, etc.)
- ✅ $500K+ annual revenue potential

### Good Prospects (B-Tier)
- ✅ Website exists but slow/mobile-unfriendly
- ✅ GBP exists but not optimized
- ✅ 10-30 reviews
- ✅ Ranking bottom of page 1
- ✅ Shows up for some keywords but not main ones

### Avoid (C-Tier)
- ❌ Already ranking #1-3 for main keywords
- ❌ 100+ reviews with 4.5+ rating
- ❌ Modern, fast website
- ❌ Clearly have SEO help already

## 📊 AUDIT SCORING SYSTEM

### Score Each Prospect (1-10):

**Website (0-3 points):**
- 0: No website
- 1: Website exists but terrible
- 2: Okay website, needs work
- 3: Good website already

**Google Business Profile (0-3 points):**
- 0: No GBP or unclaimed
- 1: GBP exists but incomplete
- 2: GBP mostly complete, needs optimization
- 3: GBP fully optimized

**Reviews (0-2 points):**
- 0: No reviews OR bad rating
- 1: Some reviews, mediocre rating
- 2: Good reviews already

**Rankings (0-2 points):**
- 0: Page 2+ or not ranking
- 1: Bottom of page 1
- 2: Top 3 already

**Total Score:**
- 0-3: 🔥 HOT LEAD ( major issues, easy wins)
- 4-6: 🟡 WARM LEAD (needs help, competitive)
- 7-10: ❄️ COLD LEAD (mostly optimized)

**Prioritize 0-3 scores.**

## 🚀 ADVANTAGES OF THIS APPROACH

### vs. Cold Calling:
- ✅ You're leading with value, not asking for anything
- ✅ They have something tangible (the audit)
- ✅ Higher response rate
- ✅ Positions you as expert immediately

### vs. Traditional SEO Pitches:
- ✅ Specific, personalized (not generic)
- ✅ Shows exactly what's wrong
- ✅ Creates urgency ("you're losing calls now")
- ✅ Differentiator from competitors

### vs. Waiting for Inbound:
- ✅ You're in control of pipeline
- ✅ Target exactly the businesses you want
- ✅ Faster results
- ✅ Scalable
