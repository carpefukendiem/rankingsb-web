## 🎯 STRATEGY 1: Pinterest SEO + Shopping

### Why It Works
- 431M Pinterest users, 46% search for products weekly
- Cushions = visual product (perfect for Pinterest)
- Long shelf-life (pins circulate for months/years)
- High buyer intent (users planning purchases)

### Implementation
**Week 1:**
- Create Pinterest Business account
- Enable Shopping features
- Create 10 boards: "DIY Cushion Ideas," "Outdoor Cushions," "Foam Replacement," etc.

**Ongoing (30 min/week):**
- Pin 5-10 product photos daily
- Use keywords in descriptions: "custom foam cushions," "outdoor seat cushions," "cushion replacement"
- Link directly to product pages
- Create "Idea Pins" showing before/after transformations

### ROI Estimate
- **Time:** 2 hrs setup, 30 min/week
- **Cost:** $0 (organic) or $50-200/mo (promoted pins)
- **Revenue:** $500-2K/mo within 90 days
- **Break-even:** Month 2

## 🎯 STRATEGY 3: Google Local Service Ads (LSA)

### Why It Works
- Pay only for qualified leads (not clicks)
- Appear at TOP of search results (above Ads)
- "Google Guaranteed" badge builds trust
- Perfect for local custom cushion searches

### Implementation
**Setup:**
- Apply for Google LSA
- Complete background check
- Set weekly budget ($300-500)
- Target: "custom cushions [city]," "foam replacement [city]"

**Optimization:**
- Respond to leads within 1 hour
- Request reviews from completed jobs
- Adjust targeting based on lead quality

### ROI Estimate
- **Time:** 2 hrs setup, 15 min/week management
- **Cost:** $15-30 per qualified lead
- **Revenue:** $2K-8K/mo (depends on lead volume)
- **Break-even:** Month 1 (if close rate is good)

## 🎯 STRATEGY 6: Retargeting Ad Campaign

### Why It Works
- 98% of website visitors don't buy on first visit
- Retargeting brings them back at 1/10th the cost of cold ads
- Visual product = high ad engagement
- Works 24/7 automatically

### Implementation
**Platforms:**
- Facebook/Instagram Retargeting
- Google Display Network
- Pinterest Retargeting

**Setup:**
- Install Facebook Pixel on website
- Create Google Ads remarketing tag
- Build 3 ad sets:
  1. **Product viewers** (didn't add to cart)
  2. **Cart abandoners** (added but didn't checkout)
  3. **Past customers** (upsell new cushions)

**Budget:** $5-10/day per platform

### ROI Estimate
- **Time:** 3 hrs setup, 30 min/week optimization
- **Cost:** $150-300/mo ad spend
- **Revenue:** $800-3K/mo
- **Break-even:** Month 1-2
- **ROAS:** 3-5x typical
