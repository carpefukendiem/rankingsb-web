## 🎬 BEST VIDEO AI ALTERNATIVES (Ranked)

### 1. 🥇 **Runway Gen-3 Alpha** (RECOMMENDED)
**Best for:** Professional marketing videos, precise control
- **Price:** $12/month (Basic) to $76/month (Pro)
- **Pros:** Industry-leading quality, precise camera control, motion brush, excellent text rendering
- **Cons:** Requires subscription, 10-second max on basic plan
- **Language:** Excellent English support
- **Best for:** The 10s and 15s marketing videos
- **Try:** runwayml.com

### 2. 🥈 **Kling AI** (BYTEDANCE)
**Best for:** Realistic motion, longer videos
- **Price:** Free tier (limited), paid plans available
- **Pros:** Extremely realistic physics, 2-minute videos, excellent motion understanding
- **Cons:** May have Chinese interface elements, less text control
- **Language:** Good English, but sometimes defaults to Chinese UI
- **Best for:** 30-second versions, lifestyle content
- **Try:** klingai.com

### 3. 🥉 **Pika Labs**
**Best for:** Creative control, anime/stylized content
- **Price:** Free tier, $8/month for standard
- **Pros:** Modify Region feature (change specific areas), excellent for product demos
- **Cons:** Can be inconsistent with text rendering
- **Language:** English primary
- **Best for:** If you need to modify specific elements
- **Try:** pika.art

### 4. **Haiper**
**Best for:** Free tier, quick generation
- **Price:** Free (generous), $10/month for priority
- **Pros:** Good quality for free, fast generation
- **Cons:** Shorter videos (4 seconds free), less control
- **Language:** English
- **Best for:** Testing concepts quickly
- **Try:** haiper.ai

### 5. **Luma Dream Machine**
**Best for:** 3D understanding, camera movement
- **Price:** Free tier, paid plans
- **Pros:** Excellent camera path control, understands 3D space
- **Cons:** Newer, less predictable
- **Language:** English
- **Best for:** Videos with complex camera movements
- **Try:** lumalabs.ai/dream-machine

3. **Be explicit about text** — Font, color, position
4. **Add negative constraints** — What NOT to include

6. **Test one first** — Don't burn credits on bad prompts
