## Penalty Avoidance

### Safe Harbor Requirements
To avoid underpayment penalties, you must have paid:
- 90% of current year tax liability, OR
- 100% of prior year tax liability (110% if prior year AGI > $150,000)

### Penalty Calculation
If underpaid:
- Penalty = Federal shortfall × 6% annual rate × time period
- CA penalty similar calculation

### Waiver Requests
Penalties may be waived if:
- Casualty, disaster, or unusual circumstance
- Retirement (age 62+) or disability
- Underpayment due to current year vs. prior year variance
