# 🌴 Pueblo Bonito Cabo San Lucas - Activity Guide
**Trip Dates:** June 22-29, 2026  
**Resort:** Pueblo Bonito (all-inclusive secured)  
**Travelers:** Ruben + Family

---

## 🎯 MUST-DO ACTIVITIES (Book These ASAP)

### 1. 🐬 **Dolphin Encounter - Cabo Adventures**
**Why:** Kids will remember this forever  
**Location:** Cabo Dolphins at the marina  
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Age:** 4+ years (6yo and 10yo perfect)  
**Price:** $129-189/person (check for family packages)

**Book at:** https://www.cabodolphins.com/
**Tip:** Book the shallow water encounter (not swim) for younger kids—safer, still amazing  
**Best time:** Early morning (less crowded, calmer water)  
**Backup:** Sea lion encounter if dolphins booked

---

### 2. 🌅 **Sunset MonaLisa Restaurant**
**Why:** #1 rated sunset dining in Cabo  
**Location:** Between Cabo San Lucas and San José (15 min from resort)  
**Vibe:** Romantic cliffside, Mediterranean-Asian fusion  
**Must-do:** Sunset reservation (arrive 30 min before)

**Book at:** https://sunsetmonalisa.com/  
**Reservations:** REQUIRED (book 2+ weeks ahead for June)  
**Price:** $$$$ ($80-120/person with drinks)  
**Kid-friendly?** Yes, but more of a special dinner  
**Alternative:** The Office (beachfront, casual, fun)

---

### 3. 🏎️ **ATV/UTV Desert Tour - Cactus ATV**
**Why:** Adventure + landscape + memorable  
**Location:** Outback trails near Migriño Beach  
**Duration:** 2-3 hours  
**Ages:** Drivers 16+, passengers any age  
**Price:** $89-149/person

**Book at:** https://www.cactusatvtours.com/  
**Includes:** Hotel pickup, gear, guided tour, margarita stop  
**Combo option:** ATV + Camel Safari (unique!)  
**Best time:** Morning (cooler, better light for photos)

---

### 4. 🏴‍☠️ **Sunset Pirate Ship Cruise - Buccaneer Queen**
**Why:** Kids LOVE this, great photos, all-inclusive drinks  
**Location:** Cabo San Lucas Marina  
**Duration:** 2.5 hours  
**Ages:** All ages (baby-friendly)  
**Price:** $65-85/adult, $35-45/kids

**Book at:** https://www.buccaneerqueen.com/  
**Includes:** Dinner, open bar, pirate show, music  
**Views:** Lands End, El Arco, sea lion colony  
**Tip:** Book early sunset cruise (5:30-8 PM)

---

### 5. 🐠 **Snorkeling at Santa Maria Bay**
**Why:** Calm water, great visibility, tons of fish  
**Location:** 20 min boat ride from marina  
**Duration:** 3-4 hours (with cruise)  
**Ages:** All ages (10mo can stay on boat with parent)  
**Price:** $45-75/person

**Book through:** 
- Pez Gato (https://pezcatocabo.com/)
- Cabo Adventures
- Or any marina tour operator

**Includes:** Gear, guide, snacks, drinks  
**Marine life:** Tropical fish, rays, maybe sea turtles  
**Best time:** Morning (calmer seas)

---

## 🎒 OTHER HIGHLY RECOMMENDED

### 6. **Zip Line Adventure - Wild Canyon**
**Location:** 20 min from Cabo San Lucas  
**Duration:** 3-4 hours  
**Ages:** 8+ for most lines (10yo can do it!)  
**Price:** $89-129/person

**Book at:** https://www.wildcanyon.com.mx/  
**Features:** Longest zip lines in Baja, hanging bridges, bungee  
**Combo:** Add UTVs or camel ride  
**Kid option:** They have a kids course for 6yo

---

### 7. **Glass Bottom Boat to El Arco**
**Why:** Cheap, quick, iconic photo op  
**Location:** Marina (walk to any booth)  
**Duration:** 1 hour  
**Price:** $15-25/person (negotiate!)

**No reservation needed**—just go to marina  
**Includes:** El Arco, sea lions, Lover's Beach, Divorce Beach  
**Tip:** Bring cash, tip the guide  
**Best time:** Morning (clear water, less wind)

---

### 8. **Horseback Riding on the Beach**
**Location:** Migriño Beach (same area as ATVs)  
**Duration:** 2 hours  
**Ages:** 6+ (6yo can ride with guide)  
**Price:** $65-95/person

**Book at:** https://www cuadra san francisco.com/ or through resort  
**Experience:** Desert trails → beach → sunset views  
**Sunset option:** Romance + photos

---

### 9. **Shopping at Puerto Paraiso Mall + Marina**
**Why:** Souvenirs, restaurants, people watching  
**Location:** Walking distance from resort  
**Cost:** Free to browse, $$ to buy  
**Features:** High-end shops, local crafts, restaurants

**Tips:**
- Haggle at craft stalls (start at 50% asking price)
- Try the tequila tastings
- Great for rainy day backup

---

### 10. **Day Trip to San José del Cabo**
**Why:** Culture, art, slower pace, great food  
**Distance:** 30 min drive from Cabo San Lucas  
**Duration:** Half day

**Must-see:**
- **Art District (Galería District):** Thursday night art walk
- **Mission San José:** Historic church
- **Flora Farms:** Farm-to-table restaurant (book ahead!)
- **Acre:** Breathtaking treehouse restaurant

**Transport:** Taxi ($40), Uber (cheaper), rental car

### Pro Tips for Baby:

- Resort likely has: Cribs, high chairs, baby food options
- Schedule around naps: Morning activity → pool → nap → dinner
- Evening: Pirate cruise or early dinner, back for bedtime

## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Tipping:** 10-15% at restaurants, $5-10 for tours, $1-2 for bags
2. **Water:** Drink bottled (resort should provide)
3. **Sun:** Baja sun is INTENSE—reapply sunscreen every 2 hours
4. **Negotiating:** Always negotiate at markets and with taxis
5. **Time Zone:** Mountain Time (same as Denver)
6. **Language:** Most places speak English, but learn "gracias" and "por favor"
7. **Safety:** Cabo is very safe for tourists, but use common sense
8. **Best Photos:** El Arco at sunset, Zip line action shots, Dolphin encounter (buy the photo package)
