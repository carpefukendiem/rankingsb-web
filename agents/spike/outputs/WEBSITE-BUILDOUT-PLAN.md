## 🎨 DESIGN RECOMMENDATIONS

### For Next.js Build (Each Page Should Have):

1. **Hero Section**
   - Dark gradient background (not white)
   - Bold headline with pain point
   - Trust badge ("Trusted by 50+ businesses")
   - Primary CTA button
   - Secondary CTA (video/learn more)

2. **Trust Signals**
   - Client logos
   - Star ratings
   - "As seen in" badges
   - Years in business

3. **Social Proof**
   - Testimonials with photos
   - Case study snippets
   - Before/after rankings
   - Video testimonials

4. **Urgency Elements**
   - "Limited spots available"
   - "Storm season coming"
   - "90-day guarantee"
   - Countdown timers (optional)

5. **Lead Capture**
   - Sticky CTA bar
   - Exit-intent popup
   - Inline forms
   - Click-to-call buttons
