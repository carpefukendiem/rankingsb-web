## 💡 PRO TIPS

### 1. Use Your Phone for Research
- Check mobile speed in real-time
- See what customers see
- Google "near me" from different locations

### 2. Screenshot Everything
- Screenshot their ranking
- Screenshot competitor rankings
- Screenshot GBP issues
- Use in audit presentation

### 3. Be Honest
- If they rank #2, say #2 (not "not ranking")
- If GBP is optimized, say so
- Don't invent problems

### 4. Find the Pain
- "You're losing to [Competitor]"
- "This is costing you calls"
- "Your customers can't find you"

### 5. Show the Gain
- "Here's what fixing this means..."
- "$X more calls per month"
- "$X,XXX in new revenue"

---

## 🚨 RED FLAGS (Don't Audit These)

**Skip if:**
- Website doesn't exist
- Business is permanently closed
- No Google presence at all
- Already a client
- Competitor is already a client

**Why:** Waste of time, won't convert

**Promises:** Quick wins (immediate value)
