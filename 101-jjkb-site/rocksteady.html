Fight Back Against Parkinson's

Rocksteady Boxing is a unique, non-contact boxing program specifically designed for people living with Parkinson's disease and other movement disorders. The program was founded on the premise that forced, intense exercise can slow the progression of Parkinson's symptoms.

At 101 Jiu Jitsu & Kickboxing, we're proud to offer Rocksteady Boxing classes in a supportive, empowering environment. Our certified coaches understand the unique challenges faced by individuals with Parkinson's and adapt exercises to meet each participant's needs.

Boxing training is particularly effective for Parkinson's because it combines aerobic exercise, coordination, balance, and cognitive engagement—all critical components for managing symptoms and improving quality of life.

Program Benefits

Research shows that forced, intense exercise can help slow the progression of Parkinson's symptoms.
