# Objection handling — Outside Rep

## “We already have someone.”
Totally fair — most of our best clients did too. The question is: are you getting *more calls month over month*, and are leads followed up instantly every time?

## “$1,500/mo is too much.”
If one new customer/patient/job covers it, it’s not expensive — it’s a growth lever. Let’s look at your average value and your close rate.

## “I need to think about it.”
Makes sense. What’s the main thing you’d need to feel 100% confident?

## “We tried marketing before.”
Most of the time it failed because there was no system: offer clarity + tracking + follow-up. We fix the system first.

## “I don’t want a long contract.”
We start with a 30-day setup and earn the month-to-month.
