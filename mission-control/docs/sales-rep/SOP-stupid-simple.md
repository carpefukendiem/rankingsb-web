# Outside Sales Rep SOP (Stupid Simple)

## The only goal
Book **qualified appointments** on this calendar:
https://link.rankingsb.com/widget/booking/RqgaExQNQEtVAr22AxVz

## Target niche (this week)
**Electricians** — Santa Barbara + Ventura County

## Daily success metrics (green / yellow / red)
- **Drop-ins:** 10 / 6 / <6
- **Decision-maker conversations:** 4 / 2 / <2
- **Booked calls:** 1 / 0 / 0

## The 5-step routine (do this every work day)
### Step 1 — Pick 10 targets (30 minutes)
- Use today’s target list (from the weekly packet)
- Prefer: weak reviews, slow follow-up vibe, outdated website, no strong offer

### Step 2 — Walk-in script (60 seconds)
**Open:**
“Hey — I’m <NAME> with RankingsB. We help electricians in SB/Ventura get more booked calls by installing a follow-up system that turns missed calls into scheduled estimates.”

**Ask:**
“Who handles your leads or scheduling? I’d love 30 seconds with them.”

If owner/manager comes out:
“Quick question: if you miss a call today, do you text them back automatically within 60 seconds?”

### Step 3 — Give the leave-behind (10 seconds)
Hand them the 1-pager. Point to:
- Proof (Freds Upholstery / Enzo MMA / Paragon Goleta)
- Booking link

### Step 4 — Log it immediately (2 minutes)
After leaving the location, log:
- Business name + city
- Who you spoke to
- Outcome (no DM / DM talked / booked / follow-up needed)
- Next step + date

### Step 5 — Same-day follow-up (end of day)
Send the follow-up text/email template to any warm conversations.

## Objections (one-liners)
- “We already have someone” → “Totally fair — this isn’t replacing them. It’s fixing the leaks: missed calls + slow follow-up.”
- “Too busy” → “That’s exactly why this works. It captures leads when you’re on a job.”
- “Price?” → “It’s $999 to install + $399/mo. We do a 3‑month minimum because we need time to prove results.”

## Weekly expectations
- 50 drop-ins/week
- 10+ decision-maker convos/week
- 3–5 booked calls/week

## What the rep does NOT do
- No pitching SEO details.
- No promising rankings.
- No custom quoting.
Just book calls.
