## Example 1: Coastal Electric (Santa Barbara)

### Quick Score
- GBP: 6/10 — good reviews (4.2★), few photos
- Website: 4/10 — slow load, no mobile booking
- Reviews: 7/10 — decent volume, not responding to negatives
- Follow-up: 2/10 — no SMS, voicemail only

### The Pitch
> "Coastal Electric has been here 15 years — you've got the reputation. But you're invisible to new customers searching 'emergency electrician Santa Barbara' at 8 PM.
>
> Three quick wins:
> 1) Add 10+ photos to your GBP (team, trucks, projects)
> 2) 5-minute response time via text (we set this up)
> 3) Review request system (get 4.5+ stars in 60 days)
>
> That's 10-15 more calls/month = $8,000-$12,000 extra revenue."
