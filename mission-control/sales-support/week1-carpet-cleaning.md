## 🔍 CARPET CLEANING AUDIT SNIPPETS

Use these during the 10-minute audit review:

### GBP Issues
- "Your Google Business Profile only has 12 reviews. [Competitor] has 200+. Each review increases call volume by about 5%."
- "You're not showing up in the 'carpet cleaning near me' map pack — 67% of people click one of those 3 results."
- "Your GBP hasn't been updated in 6 months. Google favors active profiles."

### Website Issues
- "Your website takes 6 seconds to load. 53% of mobile users leave if it takes longer than 3 seconds."
- "You have no before/after photos. Visual proof increases conversion by 40%."
- "No call tracking — you don't know which marketing is actually working."

### SEO Issues
- "You're not ranking for 'carpet cleaning [City]' — that search gets 200+ queries per month."
- "No service pages for specific services (pet stains, oriental rugs, commercial). Each is a separate search."
- "Missing FAQ schema markup — you could show up in rich snippets."

### Review Issues
- "No review generation system. Happy customers should automatically get review requests."
- "You're not responding to negative reviews — this hurts conversion by 22%."
- "No review showcase on website — social proof drives calls."
