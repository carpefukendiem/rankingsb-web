# Spike Daily Upwork Gig Finder
## Automated Job Discovery System

---

## PURPOSE
Find 10 relevant Upwork gigs every day for Ruben to apply to at 5am.

---

## DAILY ROUTINE (5 AM)

### What Happens:
1. **Spike searches Upwork** for new gigs matching criteria
2. **Filters top 10** best opportunities
3. **Saves to dashboard** with apply links
4. **Sends notification** to Ruben
5. **Ruben applies** using cover letter templates

---

## SEARCH CRITERIA

### Keywords (Rotate Daily):
- Day 1: "local SEO", "Google Business Profile", "GMB optimization"
- Day 2: "SEO audit", "website audit", "technical SEO"
- Day 3: "small business SEO", "local marketing", "SEO consultant"
- Day 4: "WordPress SEO", "on-page SEO", "content optimization"
- Day 5: "SEO strategy", "digital marketing", "search engine optimization"

### Filters:
- **Posted:** Last 24 hours only
- **Budget:** $500+ (serious clients)
- **Proposals:** Under 20 (less competition)
- **Client History:** Payment verified preferred
- **Location:** US clients preferred (higher rates)
