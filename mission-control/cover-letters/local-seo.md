```
Available for a quick call this week to discuss your goals?

Ruben Ruiz
Rankingsb.com
(805) 307-7600
```

---

## When to Use
- Client mentions "local SEO"
- Client has physical location
- Keywords like "Google Maps", "local rankings"
- Service-area businesses (plumbers, HVAC, etc.)

---

## Customization Tips
1. Replace [Client Name] with actual name
2. Replace [industry] with their business type
3. Add specific city if mentioned in job post
4. Adjust rate if job mentions budget
5. Mention specific pain points from their post
