# General SEO Cover Letter Template
## For Upwork Applications

---

## Template

```
Hi [Client Name],

I help [industry] businesses rank higher on Google and get more qualified leads.

My 90-day system includes:

**Month 1: Audit & Strategy**
- Technical SEO audit (site speed, mobile issues, errors)
- Keyword research & competitor analysis
- Content gap analysis
- Custom strategy document

**Month 2: Optimize & Build**
- On-page SEO fixes
- Content creation & optimization
- Backlink building (white-hat only)
- Local citation cleanup

**Month 3: Rank & Convert**
- Ranking improvements
- Conversion rate optimization
- Monthly reporting
- Ongoing adjustments

**Guarantee:** If you don't see measurable ranking improvements in 90 days, I work free until you do.

Recent wins:
• E-commerce site: 250% organic traffic increase
• B2B SaaS: 15 page 1 rankings (was 0)
• Local service: #1 for main keyword in 60 days

I charge $75/hour or offer fixed-price packages starting at $1,995/month.

Can I send you a free audit showing your opportunities?

Best regards,
Ruben Ruiz
Rankingsb.com
(805) 307-7600
```

---

## When to Use
- General "SEO help" requests
- Unclear scope
- Mixed local/organic focus
- When client doesn't specify exact needs

---

## Follow-Up Strategy
1. Send audit within 24 hours
2. Follow up 3 days later
3. Offer 15-minute call
4. Send case study relevant to their industry

---

## Close Rate
- **10 applications** → 1-2 responses
- **5 responses** → 1 interview  
- **3 interviews** → 1 hire

**Target:** 2-3 new clients/month from Upwork
