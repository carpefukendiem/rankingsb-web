## 🎯 INDUSTRY FOCUS: Home Services (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Handyman)

**Why Home Services Now:**
- Spring season = home improvement surge (tax refunds, warmer weather)
- Emergency services = high intent searches ("emergency plumber", "AC not working")
- Average ticket: $200-1,500+ (easily afford $297/mo)
- Repeat customers + referrals = lifetime value
- Most owners are technicians, not marketers (huge gap)

**Target Profile:**
- 2-10 employees (owner-operator or small team)
- Not ranked #1-3 for "[service] [city]"
- Google Business Profile has <20 reviews or no recent reviews
- Website is outdated or non-existent
- Local independent shops (avoid franchises like Roto-Rooter, Mr. Rooter)

**Sweet Spot Services:**
- HVAC repair/installation (highest ticket)
- Plumbing (emergency = urgent need)
- Electrical (safety concern = quick decisions)
- Handyman/general contractor (volume play)

**Opening:**
> "Hi [Name], this is Sal with Rankingsb. I'm calling because I help home service businesses get more calls from Google. Quick question — when someone's AC breaks at 2 PM on a Saturday, are they finding you or your competitor first?"

**If they say they're busy:**
> "That's exactly why I'm calling. The busy shops are the ones who need this most because you're probably turning down jobs right now. But what happens in the slow season? The guys who stay busy year-round are the ones who show up first on Google. Can I run a free 10-minute audit and show you exactly where you rank compared to [competitor name]?"

**If they say they get enough business:**
> "I hear that a lot — and that's great! Most of my best clients said the same thing. But here's what they didn't realize: they're getting the jobs that FIND them, not the jobs that are GOING to their competitors. I just audited [competitor name] and they're getting 40+ calls a month you never even see. Can I show you what that looks like?"

**To schedule audit:**
> "Perfect. The audit takes 10 minutes — I'll show you exactly where you rank, what your competitors are doing, and three quick wins you can implement today. Do you have 10 minutes right now, or should we schedule for later today?"
