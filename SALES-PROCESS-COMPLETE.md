## 📞 THE 5 DAILY CALLS: BREAKDOWN

### CALL TYPE 1: Cold Outreach (First Contact)

**Goal:** Offer free audit, book review call

**Script:**
```
"Hi [Name], this is Sal with Rankingsb. I'm calling local 
[service] companies in Santa Barbara because we're offering 
free SEO audits this week.

I analyze where you're ranking on Google, check your website, 
and compare you to your top 3 competitors.

Takes me about 10 minutes, and most companies learn something 
they didn't know.

Worth 10 minutes for me to audit [Company] and show you what 
I find?"
```

**If YES:**
- "Great! When's better for a 15-minute review — tomorrow 
  afternoon or Thursday morning?"
- Book it. Send calendar invite.

**If NO:**
- "No problem. Can I email you the audit instead? Takes 2 
  minutes to review."
- Get email. Send audit. Follow up in 2 days.

**Log in GHL:**
- Status: "Audit Scheduled" or "Audit Sent"
- Next action: Review call or Follow up

---

### CALL TYPE 2: Audit Review (The Main Event)

**Goal:** Deliver value, build trust, pitch service

**Preparation:**
- [ ] Audit printed (1 page)
- [ ] Competitor research done
- [ ] 3 specific issues identified
- [ ] ROI calculated

**The Call (15-20 minutes):**

**Minute 0-2: Rapport**
```
"Thanks for taking the time [Name]. I ran the audit on 
[Company] yesterday. Some interesting findings...

Before I dive in — how's business? Getting enough calls?"
```

**Minute 2-5: Deliver Audit (Page by Page)**
```
"Okay, here's what I found...

[Show Status section]
You're currently ranking position #[X] for '[service] Santa 
Barbara'. That's page [Y].

[Show Competitor section]
Here's who you're competing with...
[Competitor 1] is at #1 with [XX] reviews
[Competitor 2] is at #2 with [XX] reviews

Now here's what's interesting..."
```

**Minute 5-10: The 3 Issues**
```
"The good news — [positive finding].

Here are the 3 things costing you calls:

1. [Issue 1]
   [Explain simply]
   This matters because [impact]

2. [Issue 2]
   [Explain simply]
   This matters because [impact]

3. [Issue 3]
   [Explain simply]
   This matters because [impact]"
```

**Minute 10-12: The Pain**
```
"Here's what stings — [Competitor] is ranking #1 and they 
[do something worse than client]. They're getting the calls 
you should be getting.

Every month this isn't fixed, they get [X] more customers 
and you don't."
```

**Minute 12-15: The Solution**
```
"Here's the good news — all 3 issues are fixable.

The bad news — it's technical. Takes 10-15 hours to do 
right. Google's picky.

That's where we come in. We handle everything:
• Optimize your Google profile
• Fix your website issues
• Build local citations
• Create monthly content
• Track your rankings

You focus on running [Company], we get your phone ringing.

Investment is $1,995 one-time to fix everything, then $297/month 
to maintain and improve.

If this gets you just 2 extra customers per month, it pays 
for itself.

What do you think — worth exploring?"
```

**If YES (Interested):**
- "Great! Any questions about the process?"
- Answer questions
- "Ready to move forward?"
- Get payment info
- Schedule kickoff

**If MAYBE (Needs to think):**
- "Totally understand. What specifically are you weighing?"
- Address concerns
- "How about this — I'll send a proposal today, and we chat 
  Thursday?"
- Schedule follow-up

**If NO (Not interested):**
- "No problem at all. Mind if I ask what would need to change 
  for this to make sense?"
- Learn objection
- Stay in touch monthly

**Log in GHL:**
- Status: "Proposal Sent", "Negotiating", or "Closed Lost"
- Commission: $997 (if closed)

---

### CALL TYPE 3: Follow-Up (2 Days Later)

**Goal:** Answer questions, overcome objections, close

**Script:**
```
"Hi [Name], Sal following up on the SEO audit I sent over.

Did it make sense? Any questions about what I found?

[Listen]

[Address concerns]

So — worth exploring if we can get [Company] ranking higher?"
```

**If still hesitant:**
- "What would need to happen for this to be a yes?"
- "If I could [solve concern], would you move forward?"

**If timing issue:**
- "When would be better? Next month?"
- "I can hold this pricing until [date]."

Ready to get started?"

- Status: "Closed Won" 🎉 or "Closed Lost"

## 🎯 OBJECTION HANDLING QUICK REFERENCE

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "If this gets you 2 customers/month, it pays for itself. How many extra would make it worth it?" |
| "Need to think about it" | "What specifically? The investment, timing, or something else?" |
| "Already have someone" | "Are you happy with the results? Are you ranking #1?" |
| "Just send info" | "Happy to. What specifically — pricing, process, or timeline?" |
| "Not right now" | "When would be better? I can check back next month." |
| "Bad timing" | "What's your busy season? I'll call then." |
| "Need to ask partner/wife" | "Absolutely. Want me to join that conversation?" |
| "Too busy" | "I get it. This actually saves time — no more worrying about where your next customer comes from." |
| "Don't believe in marketing" | "Do you believe in customers finding you on Google? That's all this is." |
| "Tried SEO before, didn't work" | "What did they do? [Listen] We do [X, Y, Z] differently. That's why we guarantee results." |
